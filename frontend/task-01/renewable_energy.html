Energy and Renewable Resources

Bright ideas for a brighter future!

Read & Learn

Energy is what makes everything around us work. We use energy to turn on lights, ride in cars, cook food, and even play video games. But not all energy comes from the same place. Some types of energy, like coal and oil, come from deep inside the Earth. These are called non-renewable resources because once we use them up, they are gone forever. They also create smoke and gases that can harm our planet. That is why scientists and leaders all over the world are encouraging us to use renewable energy.
